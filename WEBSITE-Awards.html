NBA Awards

Most VAluable Player (MVP)

Given to the best all-around player during the regular season with the biggest impact on their team's success

Rookie of the Year

Given to the best first-year player who makes the strongest impact in their debut NBA season.

Defensive Player of the Year

Recognizes the league's top defender, judged by blocks, steals, and overall defensive presence.

Sixth Man of the Year

Awarded to the most impactful player coming off the bench, providing crucial scoring and energy.

Most Improved Player

Given to the player who shows the most noticeable improvement compared to their last seasons.

Coach of the Year

Recognizes the coach eho led their team success, often exceeding expectations.

Clutch Player of the Year

Given to the player who consistently delivers in high-pressure, late-game situations.

Finals MVP

Awarded to the best player in the NBA Finals, usually from the championship-winning team.

Championship Trophy

The Larry O'Brien Trophy, awarded to the team that wins the NBA Finals and becomes champion.
